## Missing Analysis
- The auditor missed an implementation detail regarding the interaction between `Navigation` flush events and `MacroEngine`. Because the engine enqueues steps one-by-one asynchronously, a concurrent `navigate` command will flush the scheduler queues, but it will NOT stop the `MacroEngine` from enqueuing the *next* step after the navigation. This exacerbates the State Corruption risk identified in Finding 1.
